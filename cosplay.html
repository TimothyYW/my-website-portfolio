Cosplay as a Hobby

Cosplay is a performance art where participants, known as cosplayers, wear costumes and accessories to represent a specific character from a movie, book, video game, or other media. It often involves not just the costume, but also embodying the character's personality and mannerisms. It is a creative and social hobby that connects people across cultures and fandoms.
